Sage × Bank
Open Payroll
What happens when your bank and your payroll provider finally talk to each other?
1
Employees live in their banking app
For millions of Sage customers' employees, the banking app is the first place they look when they get paid — not their payslip, not their HR portal.
2
Banks want new customers — but onboarding is broken
Banks are highly incentivised to win and retain customers. But when someone switches banks, they still have to manually notify their employer to update payroll. It's friction that costs banks customers.
3
Two trusted data sources. Zero connection.
Banks hold deeply trusted financial data and form trusted relationships with their customers. Sage holds deeply trusted employment and earnings data about their employees. Right now, these two worlds don't talk.
✨
Imagine if we brought this all together
Automatic banking detail updates on payroll. Pay insights delivered directly into the bank app. Earned wage access. A verified link between every salary payment and the employer behind it.

What happens when you tap “Connect”

In a live integration, this triggers a secure request from the bank to Sage to link the employee's profile and push updated banking details to payroll.

1
Bank sends identity requestID number + new account details sent to Sage API
2
Sage matches the employeeID linked to active payroll record at Will Bakery
3
Sage notifies the employer (HR)Will Bakery receives an approval request in their Sage HR inbox
4
Employer approves the updateHR approves → bank account updated on payroll record
5
Bank receives confirmationEmployee is notified that their details are updated
